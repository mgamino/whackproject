Tower Court Profile:

We wanted to create a web resource to help incoming and returning students find the most comfortable and accessible home in one of the campus dorms. We know that “accessible” means different things to different people, so instead of just breaking down buildings into broad categories of “accessible” or “not accessible,” we’ve provided information about specific traits and amenities of the buildings so that the students themselves can decide what is accessible for them.

Tower Court has it's own dining hall located within the building. The dining area can be entered using the stairs or the elevator.

The Tower Court dining hall provides food that is nut free.

Mobility: Clapp library is a close walk, with hills. Lulu Chow Wang Campus Center is a short walk, with one gradual decline. The Science center is the furthest academic building.

Tower has three entrances, two in the front that require stairs when you enter the building, and one in the rear that has stairs leading up to Tower.
