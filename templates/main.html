Wellesley Blueprints



Where will your home be on Wellesley's campus?

The first year of college can be a time of uncertainty and anxiety, especially if you need particular accommodations and are concerned about finding a good fit roommate and dorm. This website, Wellesley Blueprints, is designed to smooth your transition by helping you identify a niche home on campus that meets your needs.
