Beebe Hall Profile:

Food:

Dining Hall Location: Beebe is located across the residential quad from the Pomeroy dining hall. There are both stair and ramp entrances and exits on both buildings.

Food: Pomeroy's dining hall is both kosher and vegetarian. It also consistently provides vegan options.


Mobility:

Outside the Building:

Beebe is across the quad from Pomeroy, and can be accessed by sidewalk or by walking across the grass.

Beebe is a moderate distance from the library, and involves walking up and down hills. The most direct route is past the Lulu Chow Wang campus center and along the sidewalk passing Severence Green.

Beebe is relatively close to the campus center; it is at the base of the two hill entrances/exits to the quad, across the street.

Gradation and Hills: Beebe has two main approaches. One is a short & fairly steep (but smoothly paved) path that leads to a few steps at the building entrance. The other approach is a longer but less steep paved path leading to an entrance with a ramp.

Inside the Building:

Elevator: Beebe has a small, fairly narrow elevator that is immediately inside of the front door entrance and down the 1st floor hallway from the ramp entrance. One significant impedimant to mobility in Beebe's basement, though, is two short flights of steps that must be climbed to get from the portion of the basement level where the elevator opens to the portion of the basement with both a larger kitchen and the laundry room.

Doorknobs: Beebe has TK style doorknobs.

Amenities:

Laundry Beebe has laundry in the basement, but, as described above, students must go up and down a few steps in order to access the room.

Restroom: The first floor in Beebe has an acessible restroom.

Kitchen: Beebe has a kitchen on each floor. On some floors the kitchen is divided, but on others it is just one room. In the basement, there is a larger kitchen and a dining table.

Sound and Alarms:

Fire Alarms: TBD - flashing lights in some rooms? True in Shafer...

Heaters: The heaters in Beebe, as in most dorms on campus, are fairly loud. They "clang and bang" regularly beginning in the fall.

Environnment:

Allergens: Beebe has hardwood flooring, no air-conditioning, and heating in every room.
